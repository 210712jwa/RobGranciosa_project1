What is an Expense Reimbursement?


Employees frequently pay company expenses - such as lodging, travel, meal, or entertainment expenses - out of their own pocket, and then ask for reimbursement.


What is an expense reimbursement? Generally speaking, to qualify as an expense reimbursement:

  • The expense must be for deductible business expenses that are paid or incurred by an employee in the course of performing services for Rob & Co.
  • You must be able to substantiate the amount, time, use, and business purpose of the reimbursed expenses.
